Multi-Task Learning（多任务学习）

Posted on 2020-01-12 | Post modified: 2020-01-12 | |

多任务学习介绍和代码实现。

概念

（1）定义

Multi-task learning (MTL) is a subfield of machine learning in which multiple learning tasks are solved at the same time, while exploiting commonalities and differences across tasks. This can result in improved learning efficiency and prediction accuracy for the task-specific models, when compared to training the models separately.
多任务学习是同时学习多个任务，简单说就是有多个loss 同时训练。相比于单个模型单个loss，该方式能够提高模型的performance，当然使用的条件是任务之间具有相关性。多任务学习有很多别名，如联合学习（Joint Learning），自主学习（Learning to Learn），借助辅助任务学习（Learning with Auxiliary Tasks）。

（2）多任务学习的分类

1). 参数的硬共享机制

多任务学习中最常见的一种形式。在所有任务的隐藏层共享参数，保留任务相关的输出层，硬共享机制降低了过拟合的风险，越多任务同时学习，过拟合的风险越低。

（4）多任务学习的优势

1). 多个相关的任务，有相通的地方有不同的部分。当学习一个任务的时候，容易学习到数据的噪声（过拟合），但是添加新的任务，能够加强任务之间的共性，减少过拟合的程度，提高泛化性能。

2). > It’s one of the most interesting and exciting areas of research for Machine Learning in coming years, radically reducing the amount of data required to learn new concepts. One of the great promises of Deep Learning is that, with the power of the models and simple ways to share parameters between tasks, we should be able to make significant progress in multi-task learning.
对于特定的一个细分领域，减少训练数据量的需求

（2）Alternate Training

Alternate Training 是轮流训练不同的任务

开/合

Tips: When is Alternate Training Good?

For example, in vision, you might want one of your tasks to predict the rotation of an object, the other what the object would look like if you changed the camera angle. These two tasks are obviously related - in fact the rotation probably comes before the image generation.
预测图片中物品的角度和物体（if you changed the camera angle），这两个任务是相关的。

Tips: When is Alternate Training Less Good?

Alternate training can easily become biased towards a specific task. The first way is obvious - if one of your tasks has a far larger dataset than the other, then if you train in proportion to the dataset sizes your shared layer will contain more information about the more significant task.
当两个任务包含的数据集大小比例相差比较大的时候，模型对于大数据集的信息包含更多。

（3）Training at the Same Time - Joint Training

开/合

Tips: When is Alternate Training Less Good?

The first thing we tried was simply to sum the different losses. Soon enough we could see that while one task converges to good results, the others look pretty bad. When taking a closer look, we could easily see why. The losses’ scales were so different, that one task dominated the overall loss, while the rest of the tasks didn’t have a chance to affect the learning process of the shared layers.
简单相加容易发生一个model 主导了整个的情况
A quick fix was replacing the sum of losses with a weighted sum, that brought all losses to approximately the same scale. However, this solution involves another hyperparameter that might need to be tuned every once in a while.
一种解决方案是有权重相加，但是引入了超参数
Luckily, we found a great paper proposing to use uncertainty to weigh losses in MTL. The way it is done, is by learning another noise parameter that is integrated in the loss function for each task. This allows having multiple tasks, possibly regression and classification, and bringing all losses to the same scale. Now we could go back to simply summing our losses.
更近一步连超参数都不用了

总结

Multi-task learning is helpful when you want to perform similar tasks using same network. This not only reduces training time but also reduces inference time as compared with performing prediction with two different models.
相比于多个单任务的训练和预测，多任务学习在时间和使用gpu空间上都是优于前者的。

It can be generalized to more complex problems like recognizing facial expressions and determining person face attributes.
对于任务相关的模型，可以防止过拟合，得到更好的训练模型的效果。
